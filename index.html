About Us

History: Munder Difflin Paper Corp., parent company to Dunder Mifflin is a family run paper sales company located in the heart of New York City. Munder Difflin was founded in 1949 by Robert Dunder to help local bussiness streamline their operation. Mission: To improve people’s lives, the planet and our company's performance by transforming renewable resources into products people depend on every day. Values: To be among the most successful, sustainable and responsible companies in the world
